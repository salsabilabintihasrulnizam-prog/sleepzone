ABOUT SLEEPZONE

SleepZone is a sleep monitoring website designed to help users understand and improve their sleeping habits. By recording daily sleep logs, users can track important information such as sleep time, wake up time, sleep quality, mood upon waking, screen time before bed, caffeine intake, exercise level, naps, and personal notes.

The platform transforms sleep records into meaningful insights that are easy to understand. Users can view their average sleep duration, sleep score, and weekly sleep trends through visual charts and summaries. SleepZone also provides a sleep history feature that allows users to review both current and previous sleep records in one place.

In addition, SleepZone includes a sleep calculator that provides recommended sleep durations based on age. Users can calculate suitable bedtimes or wake up times according to their desired amount of sleep. The website also offers sleep ambience features, practical sleep tips, and helpful guidance to encourage healthier sleeping habits and overall wellbeing.

WHY SLEEPZONE

SleepZone was developed for people who struggle with poor sleep habits, irregular sleep schedules, insomnia, stress, and fatigue. Many individuals experience periods where sleep is affected by academic deadlines, work pressure, personal responsibilities, or emotional challenges.

By helping users track their sleep over time, SleepZone makes it easier to identify patterns that may affect sleep quality. Users can compare stressful periods, busy schedules, healthier routines, and lifestyle changes to better understand how their daily activities influence their rest.

Whether you are facing examination pressure, work stress, personal challenges, or simply trying to build healthier habits, SleepZone provides a safe space to monitor your progress. The goal is to help users become more aware of their wellbeing and take small steps towards healthier sleep habits every day.
