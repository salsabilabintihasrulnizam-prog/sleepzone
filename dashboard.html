Sleep Tips

Small habits can make a big difference in sleep quality, energy levels, and overall well-being.

🌙

Follow a Consistent Sleep Routine

Try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, including weekends. A regular schedule helps your body maintain a healthy sleep pattern.

🏃

Stay Active During the Day

Regular physical activity can improve sleep quality. Avoid intense workouts too close to bedtime as they may make it harder to fall asleep.

☕

Reduce Stimulants Before Bed

Limit caffeine and nicotine, especially in the afternoon and evening, since they can keep your mind and body alert when you should be winding down.

📖

Create a Relaxing Bedtime Routine

Spend some time unwinding before sleep through calming activities such as reading, listening to soft music, or taking a warm bath.

🛏️

Make Your Bedroom Sleep-Friendly

Keep your sleeping environment cool, quiet, dark, and comfortable. Minimize distractions from bright lights and electronic devices.

☀️

Get Enough Natural Light

Exposure to sunlight helps regulate your body's internal clock, making it easier to stay alert during the day and sleep well at night.
